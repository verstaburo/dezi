Redstone
Hotel Logo

A new boutique hotel in the centre of Old Town Riga was named Redstone in honour of an unusual and colourful architectural design. Despite the fact that the building is new, it fits perfectly into the historic centre of the city. The agency has developed a logo for the new hotel.

The Secret

The boutique hotel Redstone has 11 rooms, which are differently designed. The sign, which is part of the Redstone inscription, hides in place of the letter N and consists of two inverted 1 digits, personifying 11 hotel rooms, house number 11 and the desire to be the first everywhere and in everything.
