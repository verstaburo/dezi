Website and logo for the booking agency Next Level Agency

Next Level Agency is an international booking agency that organizes concerts, festivals and private events in the entertainment industry. We’ve created a bright, trendy and dynamic website.

Case

  • Logo design
  • Website development

Responsive web design

Booking of the artists should be available anywhere in the world 24 hours a day right away as it is not known in which part of the world there will be a concert tomorrow. Responsive website design helps to do that.

The new logo of the agency

The new website of the agency needed a new vision of the company's corporate identity. We have created a new, bright logo that fully reflects the essence of the company's work, which is a bit different from others in everything.

Portraits of agency managers

The customer wanted concise illustrations of the company's employees instead of photos, so we drew every booking manager in the right style.
